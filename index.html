Baked Pesto Salmon

Mix together store-bought pesto and dijon mustard (about 50/50) in a bowl and spread generously over salmon filets. Bake on 350 degrees for 15 minutes (a little more if you like it more well done)

Moroccan Stew

Cut 3/4 pounds of filet mignon into smaller squares. Also chop up 1 head of cauliflower, 3 large carrots, 1 yellow onion and a few cloves of garlic. Brown the meat in a pan and then remove and Add onion

Chicken Stir Fry

Slice 2 chicken breasts into strips or cubes. Brown the chicken in a pan and set aside. Chop 2 heads of broccoli, 2 zucchinis, 1 small piece of ginger and 1 garlic clove. Saute together with olive oil
